Space Quiz

INTRODUCTION

Knowledge is power. Be it trivial or noteworthy, it controls access to opportunity and advancement. You never know when you will need a particular piece of information. Sometimes those pieces of information which we consider worthless can solve inordinate puzzles of the universe! National Students Space Challenge 2021 is providing young minds with an opportunity to showcase their knowledge by the most exciting and fiercest space quiz in India! Seize this opportunity to compete against the very best in India in conquering the throne of knowledge.

RULES AND REGULATIONS

  • There will be two categories for this event: school category and college category
  • The preliminary round will comprise three online MCQ based quizzes. The teams may participate in one or more of these. In case a team wishes to participate in more than one quiz, the best score out of them will be chosen. The preliminary round will be common for both categories.
  • The qualifiers of the preliminary round will proceed to the prefinal round which will be conducted during the fest. The 8 best teams will qualify for the final round where they will battle it out against each other to win the ultimate prize. The prefinal and final rounds will be separate for the two categories.
  • The maximum team size is 3. The team members may not necessarily be from the same institution.
  • The top 3 teams from each category will be awarded by Certificate of Excellence for the event.
  • Prize money worth 30,000 INR will be distributed among the winning teams.
